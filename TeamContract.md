# Team Contract

## Purpose of this Contract

This contract sets out shared expectations and commitments for how our team will collaborate during the course. It is designed to promote accountability, professionalism, and mutual support as we complete individual preparation, readiness assurance tests, in-class activities, and our course project.

## Team Norms and Expectations

### Communication

We will use a discord group chat to communicate. We will respond to messages in within 48 hours.

We agree to notify one another if we are going to miss a lecture or are struggling with a deadline.

To foster respectful conversation we will by being constructive and professional.

## Decision Making

We will make decisions by majority vote. If a vote is needed, all members are expected to vote by our standard communication response time (48 hours).

## Conflict resolution

If conflict arises:

- The team will first attempt to resolve the issue privately through open discussion.
- If the issue persists, the team will involve a member of the course teaching team as mediator.

## Accountability

- Complete their share of the work in a timely manner
- Seeking assistance from when required
- All members agree to provide honest and fair peer evaluations
